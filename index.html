XP Planner Mobile Application

Many people struggle with staying motivated and organized in everyday tasks, especially those who have been diagnosed with ADHD. While there are apps available to help track and manage schedules, many of these apps lack any sort of reward or engagement for their users. The "XP Planner" mobile application plans to address this issue by giving users a sense of reward for their efforts to stick to their schedules. The mobile app turns a schedule into a game, rewarding users with levels and achievements for every completed task. This mobile application plans to keep the user more engaged and satisfied when sticking to a schedule.
